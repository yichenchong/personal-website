About Me
I grew up in a diverse international background, and consequently, developed a wide variety of interests. I'm a huge tech enthusiast, and I love to solve problems and code (and especially both at once). My tech interests range from data science to full-stack development, and I also engage in engineering, design, and business.

I'm an incredibly fast hands-on learner, with a penchant for picking up new skills. If you want someone who has great test-taking and right-answer-regurgitation abilities, that's not me. However, if you want someone who can take projects and execute on them or who can generate creative solutions to issues, I'm your man.

I'm always looking for new opportunities to extend and use my skills, so if my background sounds like a good fit for you, contact me and let me know!
