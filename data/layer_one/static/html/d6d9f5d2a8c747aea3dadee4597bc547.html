As part of my National Service, I was enlisted into the Singaporean military, where I served as a sergeant in the combat engineers. I oversaw a section of 5 men in combat, peacetime, and administrative duties.
Achievements
  • Upon completion of Basic Military Training, selected to and completed Specialist Cadet School (SCS), a military leadership institute where I trained to become a non-commissioned officer
  • Received further training, such as the Armskote Management Course
  • Earned 3 badges during my service
    • Individual Physical Proficiency Test (IPPT) Gold
    • Advanced Trainfire Package (ATP) Marksman
    • Basic Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD)
Key Responsibilities
  • Operated and maintained heavy equipment for combat fortification and construction
  • Participated in both demolition and ordnance disposal exercises
  • Developed training and tactic doctrine materials
  • Oversaw training and evaluation of soldiers in combat engineer taskings
  • Managed the administration and welfare of soldiers
  • Led soldiers in peacetime operational roles (i.e. protecting national parades, camp guard duties, etc.)
  • Carried out logistic duties, such as the management of the armskote (company armoury)
